Cloud Search

The information you need, right when you need it

  • Use the power of Google to search across your company’s content. From Gmail and Drive to Docs, Sheets, Slides, Calendar, and more, Google Cloud Search answers your questions and delivers relevant suggestions to help you throughout the day.
Get started
PLAY VIDEO

The best of Google Search for your business

Cloud Search, available as part of Google Workspace, can help bring the best of enterprise search to your business by helping you quickly and easily find relevant data and files.

Play Demo Video

Watch Webinar

Privacy and security that meets your needs

Search results reflect the security models of your organization as it changes over time. Cloud Search supports near-instant updates to group access — so users only see search results for content they have access to.

Seamlessly integrated with Google Workspace

Cloud Search is seamlessly integrated with other Google Workspace apps. Search across Gmail, Docs, Drive, Calendar, and more.

Rapid deployment

With powerful web and mobile apps, teams can start using Cloud Search in no time.

Find people faster

Search your company directory. See colleagues’ contact details, plus events and files you have in common.

Available in Google Workspace Business and Enterprise editions

Cloud Search is available for customers using Google Workspace Business and Enterprise editions.

Top questions about Cloud Search

Does Cloud Search support third-party data?

Yes, Cloud Search includes connectors to third-party data sources such as Salesforce, SAP, and more than 100 other data sources.

Is Cloud Search available without a Google Workspace license?

No, Cloud Search is included with select editions of Google Workspace. Learn more here.

Does it search across all Google Workspace apps?

Cloud Search currently supports most Google Workspace services. See our help center for more information.
