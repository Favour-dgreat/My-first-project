Partnering with the public sector to build a better future

What we do

Google has been working with government and education since 2006. That year, we expanded our offerings beyond consumers, launching solutions purpose-built for enterprises.

In 2022, we officially launched Google Public Sector, a separate subsidiary that focuses on helping U.S. public sector institutions accelerate their digital transformations to meet their mission. We continue to make significant investments and grow our team to meet the increasingly complex needs of government and education.

Who we serve

Federal Civilian

From the NIH to USPS to USPTO, federal civilian agencies work with Google to increase operational effectiveness, deliver proven innovation, and solve for mission success through digital transformation.

Learn more

Defense and Intelligence

We are proud to support the critical missions of our military and national security personnel around the world by providing them with secure, reliable, innovative cloud solutions.

Learn more

State and Local Government

We help state and local government agencies improve the constituent and workforce experience in areas like health and human services, public benefits, transportation, climate, and research.

Learn more

Education

We’re committed to advancing learning for everyone. We provide cloud solutions, teaching tools, educational programs and affordable devices that help transform classrooms, academic institutions, research, and edtech companies.
